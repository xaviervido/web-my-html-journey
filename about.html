About Me

Discovering the Joy of Web Development

Who Am I?

Hi there! My name is David Osmau, and I’m an aspiring web developer. My journey into the world of coding began as a challenge to myself. Starting from absolute zero knowledge, I’ve worked my way through the struggles of understanding HTML and CSS. Today, I’m proud to share my story of transformation.

Why I Started Learning Web Development

It all began with curiosity. I wanted to create something meaningful on the web, but I had no idea where to start. After countless Google searches, I stumbled upon freeCodeCamp.org, a platform that changed everything for me. Through their structured curriculum, I discovered the magic of HTML and CSS.

What I Love About Coding

The most fulfilling part of coding is turning ideas into reality. Every line of code brings a design to life, and every solved bug feels like a small victory. Coding is more than just a skill; it’s a way to express creativity and problem-solving.

What’s Next for Me?

I’m excited to continue growing as a developer. My next steps include mastering JavaScript, learning responsive design, and eventually diving into full-stack development. The journey never ends, and I’m here for it!
